Kitchen Renovation Brisbane Cost Explained

Key takeaway

Kitchen renovation Brisbane cost typically ranges from $30,000 to $80,000 depending on scope, materials, and finishes.

Budget kitchen renovation: $15,000-$25,000
Mid-range kitchen renovation: $30,000-$50,000
Premium kitchen renovation: $60,000+
Typical project duration: 8-12 weeks

Common questions

What is the average cost of a kitchen renovation in Brisbane? Most Brisbane homeowners spend $30,000-$50,000 on mid-range kitchen renovations including new cabinetry, benchtops, and appliances. Budget options start at $15,000, while premium projects exceed $60,000.

How can I reduce kitchen renovation costs? Reduce kitchen renovation Brisbane cost by choosing modular over custom cabinetry, selecting mid-range materials over premium options, keeping existing layouts to minimise structural changes, and obtaining competitive quotes from licensed builders.
